## **1. Status Summary**

This specification is currently **inactive** for MVP1 due to scope consolidation and deferral.

### **1.1 Job Payments (Consolidated)**
The "Job Payments" portion of this original concept (Homeowner → Contractor payments) has been fully consolidated into **SPEC-004: Invoicing & Payments (MVP1)**.
- See `SPEC-004` Section 2.3 (PAID Status) and Section 5 (Stripe Integration).

### **1.2 SaaS Billing (Deferred)**
The "SaaS Billing" portion (Contractor → Platform subscription payments) is explicitly **Out of Scope** for MVP1.
- Reference: **MVP1 MASTER PRD**, Section 3.2 (Out of Scope), "Subscriptions & billing plans".

## **2. Future Roadmap**

When SaaS Billing is prioritized for MVP2 or later, this specification will be revived to cover:
- Contractor subscription tiers
- Platform usage fees
