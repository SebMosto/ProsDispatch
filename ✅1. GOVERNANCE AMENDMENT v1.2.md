# **🏛️ Governance Amendment v1.2**

### ***SaaS Billing, Contractor Vaulting & 20% Referral Engine (6-Month Cash Payout Model)***

## **Purpose**

This amendment updates the canonical architecture to:

1. Add **Contractor Subscription Billing**

2. Add **Contractor Vaulting** (safe, Stripe Billing only)

3. Add **20% Referral Engine for 6 months** (Cash payouts via Connect Transfers)

4. Preserve **Segregation of Vaulting** for legal/compliance

5. Add payout safety rules (Net-30, Fund-Cleared)

6. Update MVP1 scope & future architecture

---

# **🔒 Amended Canonical Rules**

## **Rule 1 — Contractor Billing Model (SaaS)**

ProsDispatch is a subscription SaaS for contractors.  
 Contractors **must** maintain an active subscription to access platform features.

Subscription states:

* `trialing`

* `active`

* `past_due`

* `unpaid`

* `canceled`

**Contractor Vaulting is Allowed**  
 (via Stripe Customer object \+ PaymentMethod)

---

## **Rule 2 — Segregation of Vaulting (MANDATORY)**

| Feature | Contractor | Homeowner |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Save card | ✔ Allowed | ❌ Forbidden |
| Stripe Customer | ✔ Required | ❌ Forbidden |
| Recurring billing | ✔ Allowed | ❌ Forbidden |
| Guest checkout | ✔ Allowed for jobs | ✔ Required |

This prevents illegal money-handling patterns and ensures Connect compliance.

---

## **Rule 3 — Referral Engine (MANDATORY)**

A contractor earns **20% of another contractor’s subscription price for 6 months** after referral.

* Commission is paid **monthly** on successful subscription invoices.

* Commission uses **Stripe Connect Transfers** (Option A – Cash Payouts).

* Referral commissions **never** reduce homeowner payments.

* Referral commissions **never** interfere with job payments.

* Referral commissions are **separate revenue stream \#2**.

---

## **Rule 4 — Payout Safety Rules (MANDATORY)**

To avoid negative balances:

* Payouts operate on **Net-30** OR “funds available”

* Payouts must only use **Dispatch platform balance**

* Payouts must match Stripe’s available balance constraints

* No instant payout logic allowed

---

## **Rule 5 — Plan Management**

Creating & editing subscription plans **must** be done in the **Stripe Dashboard**.

The app:

* Fetches active prices

* Displays plan cards

* Redirects to Checkout & Billing Portal

The app does **not**:

* Create plans

* Modify prices

* Build plan editors

---

## **Rule 6 — Access Control**

To use ProsDispatch:

* `billing_status = active || trialing || past_due`

* Otherwise → Contractor is blocked with “Subscription Required” screen

---

## **Rule 7 — Immutable Separation of Revenue Streams**

1. **Dispatch → Contractor**  
    SaaS Subscription Billing (Stripe Billing)

2. **Contractor → Homeowner**  
    Job Payments (Stripe Connect — Direct Charges only)

3. **Dispatch → Referrers**  
    Referral Commissions (Stripe Connect Payouts)

These flows must never overlap.

---
